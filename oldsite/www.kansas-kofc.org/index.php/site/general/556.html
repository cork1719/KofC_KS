Health Services

These programs promote the health of our members and families and could save a life. The State Council encourages all members to participate in th Health Services Program. Councils should plan on holding or hosting one or more of the following activities:

- Blood Donor Program
- Blood Pressure Clinic
- CPR Training
- Organ Donor Program

Councils can show their appreciation to participants with certificates or other recognition for their unselfish and caring efforts that may have helped to save someone’s life.
