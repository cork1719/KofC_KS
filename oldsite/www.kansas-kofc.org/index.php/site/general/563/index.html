Golf Tournament

The State Golf Committee extends an invitation to all Knights and their wives to join in the fraternity, fellowship and fun at our annual State Knights of Columbus Golf Tournament. A paid-up Traveling/Membership Card is required for entry. Wives of members in good standing are eligible and encouraged to participate.

District 18 will host the 2016 fraternal year Kansas State Knights of Columbus Golf Tournament.  Any Council interested in hosting the 2017 Kansas State Knights of Columbus Golf Tournament MUST MAKE their request in writing to Michael Grothoff, Council Activities Director before January 16, 2016.
