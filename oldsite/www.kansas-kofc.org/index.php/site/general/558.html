State Website

The Kansas State Council maintains a website with many resources for all members in Kansas. The goal of the website is to provide current information to facilitate communication between the State Council and council officers, members and guests.

Information on the website includes a calendar of events (major degrees and exemplification dates, form due dates), state programming forms, information on the Knights in Kansas, and much more. There is also an area to sign up for email notices from the State Council.
