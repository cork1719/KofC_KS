Prayer for Vocations, the Unborn, and the Fr. Michael J. McGivney Cause

Gracious God, we are your people embraced by your love. We thank you for your presence with us throughout all time.

You know each of us and you call us by name to serve you in faith.

We pray for those whom you have called to serve as priests, Brothers and Sisters, those You are calling now, and those You will call in the future. May they be open and responsive to the call of serving your people.

We pray, too, for the helpless unborn. You create men in Your own image, and You desire that not even the least among us should perish.

In Your love for us, You entrusted Your only Son to the holy Virgin Mary.

Now, in Your love, protect against the wickedness of the devil, those little ones to whom You have given the gift of life.

And finally, we pray for the beatification of Fr. Michael J. McGivney. You called your servant to be an apostle of Christian family life and to lead the young to the generous service of their neighbor.

Let the inspiration of his work of caring for the needy and the outcast prompt us to greater confidence in your love.

We humbly ask that You glorify your servant, Fr. Michael J. McGivney, on earth according to the design of Your holy will.

We ask this through Christ our Lord. Amen.

(Compiled by Mrs. Ruth Baalmann, wife of Past State Deputy Bill Baalmann)
