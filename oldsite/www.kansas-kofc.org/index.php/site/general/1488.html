Incentives

Individual Members –The top ten individual recruiters in each region will receive a prize for his efforts – first place winners will receive $150, second place winners $100 and third place winners $50. The 4th – 10th place winners will receive Knights of Columbus portfolios. Supreme offers rewards for individual recruitment efforts. Please visit the website www.kofc.org for details.

Councils – Every council that holds a first degree will have their name put into a monthly drawing to win a grilling apron that states “Everything Tastes Better When Cooked by a Knight.” Each council that achieves 100% of membership quota or more will receive a “Kansas State Membership Club 2015” shirt with the Knights of Columbus logo. Any council who achieve a net gain in the fraternal year of 100 or more will receive the Century Club Award and be awarded a $1000 U.S. Saving Bond. In addition again this year, to help get off to a good start the $5.00 state per capita will be credited for each new or reactivated members taken in during July, August and September of 2014. Supreme offers the following council awards:

·    The Father McGivney Award for achievement of membership quota

·    The Founder’s Award for achievement of insurance quota

·    The Columbian Award for success in programming and submission of report form SP7 by June 30, 2015 and the Annual Survey of Fraternal Activity (form 1728 which is due on January 31, 2015)

·    Star Council Award for achievement of the above awards

·    Double Star Award for the achievement of the Star Council Award with 200% of net gain in membership.

·    Triple Star Award for the achievement of the Star Council Award with 300% of net gain in membership.

District Deputies also significantly drive success and as such have the potential to earn the following rewards.

District Deputies – each District Deputy who achieves 100% of membership quota or more will receive a “Kansas State Membership Club 2015” pullover. Each District Deputy that achieves 150% of membership quota or more will receive a “Kansas State Membership Club 2015” shirt with the Knights of Columbus logo. District Deputies who achieve a gross intake during the fraternal year of 10% of the district membership on July 1 of 2015 will receive a men’s Knights of Columbus gold Skagen watch. When a new council is chartered, the District Deputy responsible will receive a plaque with a copy of the council’s charter on it.

·    Star District Award for achievement of 100% of the district membership and insurance goals.
.    Double Star District Award for achievement of insurance goal and 200% of the membership goal.
.    Triple Star District Award for achievement of insurance goal and 200% of the membership goal.
·    All Star District Award achieved when all councils in the district receive Star Council Awards for which the District Deputy will receive an All Star District personalized jacket.
