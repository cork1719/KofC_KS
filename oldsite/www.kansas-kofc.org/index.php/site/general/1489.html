Membership Contests

Council

Great start – the state per capita of $5.00 is credited for every new or reactivated member in July through September.
Serve the Church – a council receives 20 points for taking in a member in August, 15 points for a member in September and 10 points for a member in October. If a councils takes in a member in each of the three months they receive 25 bonus points and a council receives 20 points for each first degree they host during the three month period. Councils will compete in their membership division and receive 5,000 VIP points for the most points in their membership division.

Second half contests will be announced at the mid year meeting,

District

Degrees, Faith, Family, Fraternity – District Deputies compete in one of six divisions based on the size of their division and receives points for hosting first degrees in their district. For every first degree hosted in your district July through December, 20 contest points are awarded. In those district where there is at least one first degree in the district in each month July through December, 100 bonus points are awarded. The winner in each division is the District Deputy with the most contest points. The overall winner is the District Deputy with the most contest points.

Divisional winners receive a Knights of Columbus ink pen.
The overall winner receives a Knights of Columbus wrist watch.

Second half contests will be announced at the Mid Year meeting.
