Former Supreme Chaplain Most Reverend Thomas V. Daily, Bishop Emeritus of Brooklyn

(05-17-2017) We regret to inform you that Former Supreme Chaplain Most Reverend Thomas V. Daily, Bishop Emeritus of Brooklyn, New York, passed away on May 14,2017. He was 89 years old. Please pray for the repose of his soul and for the consolation of his family and friends. May he rest in the peace of Christ, the Risen Lord.
