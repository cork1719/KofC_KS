How Do I Join?

A practical Catholic, male, who has attained his eighteenth birthday, is eligible to join the Knights of Columbus.

If you are interested in joining the Knights of Columbus, please contact the council nearest you for more information. If you are not sure which councils are near by, you may search all councils in the state of Kansas by city name by clicking here.
