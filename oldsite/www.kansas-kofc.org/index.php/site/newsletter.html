Explorer Newsletters

"The Explorer" is the monthly newsletter of the Kansas State Council that is sent to all state personnel, District Deputies and Grand Knights. It is offered here on this website so that all Kansas Knights may be kept informed of Knights programs and happenings. You need Acrobat Reader to view these issues.
